Our Services

Sanitation

To improve sanitation standards by conducting general awareness programmers, implementing solid waste management projects, constructing toilets etc

Old Age

To educate the young and old alike about their rights and duties enshrined in the constitution of India so that they can avoid offence and defend the needy against offence/oppression by state or non state actors.

Children Welfare

To establish and develop an institution for the differently abled children to provide them education, food, clothes or other help

MGNR Services

To establish/provide financial assistance for maintaining charitable institutions such as Maternity homes, Women and child welfare centers, Houses for the poor and Orphanages.

Medical Camp

To undertake welfare activities including Medical and Diagnostic camps and Treatment for differently abled persons and also other rehabilitation activities.
